## Different types of RTR receiver board

a. XX-170R - non proportional. works with 2 pin toys gearbox type of servo


b. XX-162R - proportional. works with standard 3 pin servo

c. XX-184 - new non-proportional receiver board

d. XX-318R - old D12 receiver board


e. XX-339R - new D12 receiver board



f. XX-363T - D12 Remote board



g. XX-412R - new proportional receiver for B & C series RTR. Release Year 2022.

## There are 2 liquids in the bag. What are they?

Grease - Square shape bag with blue color substance. for use on axle



Loctite - A tube with either red or blue liquid. for use on screws where metal meets metal

* Red is a very strong Loctite, apply very minimal drop only!

## Can my RTR take 2S Lipo/Li-Ion

It depends on which board is on your RTR. Look for the below model code printed on your receiver.



a. XX-170R board - No


b. XX-162R board - Yes


Does both the boards above support 3S?

- NO!

## RES-V3 Light Function

Headlight - always on. When signal lost, it blinks


Tail light - lights up as you Reverse

Side indicators - lights blink as you steer left or right

Note : Front light can be turned off with long press of spk/light button

## Does KIT & KM include Electronics?

No. KIT and KM doesn't include any electronics unlike the RTR. You will need the electronics below,

- RES-V3 aka Sound System V3 - All in one Electronics
- Li-Ion 7.4V Battery
- 2S 7.4V USB Charger

## Will V1 tires fit on C34 C44 wheels

Yeap it does fit although C34 C44 wheel diameter is slightly bigger(2mm) and C44 has taller tires.
